Alan Watts

The self-styled "philosophical entertainer".

Timeline of Alan Watts' life:

  • 1915: Born outside London
  • 1938: Moved to the United States & became an Episcopal Priest
  • 1945: Became an Episcopal Priest
  • 1951: Wrote his pivotal book The Wisdom of Insecurity: A Message for an Age of Anxiety.
  • 1951: Moved to San Francisco where he began teaching Buddhist studies
  • 1956: Began his popular radio show, “Way Beyond the West.”
  • 1973: Wrote and traveled regularly until his passing in 1973.

If you would like, you can learn more at the Alan Watts Organization.
